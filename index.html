Who we are





Hello! We are Vincent Grenfeldt (Rajkkor) and Karl Pedersen (The_Blahblahblah), two professional game designers and content creators from Scandinavia.
We have long had a passion to create the best gaming experiences in Minecraft, our extensive experience over 14 years combined enables us to plan, build execute resulting in designs to replicate the same impression from our childhood we first had when we started playing the fun games we all enjoy.
We currently work and publish with Blockception on the Minecraft Marketplace.

Our Workflow






Building & Construction

We have a drive for exploration, and it's our philosophy when designing our maps, never letting the player feel any boredom.
We make sure that their time spent in our games is of great quality.


Modelling



Standing out is an important expect of every game. we make sure to not only give the players a unique experience in the enviornment,
but also in their way of interacting with it with custom models and textures to bring life to our maps.



Development & Scripting


Interacting with the game is very important, We therefore make sure to enhance the experience from static looks to dynamic experience with custom behaviour to give the players a toolbox to experiment and explore new ways to play.

And we make sure to keep you updated on the progress!

Working with roadmaps to plan and execute.

And Deliver consistent quality. and we make sure to push the boundries of what can be fun in Minecraft. Have a look at Patch Note worthy Super Racers!
